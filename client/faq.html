Frequently Asked Questions

Financial Aid

How do I apply for financial aid?
Open the UniSavvy app, go to the Financial Aid section, and follow the step-by-step application guide provided.
Who is eligible for financial aid?
Eligibility varies by university, but most aid is based on income level, enrollment status, and residency.

Student Discounts

Where can I find student discounts?
Check the Discounts section of UniSavvy for updated deals on food, transport, tech, and more.
Do I need a student card for discounts?
Yes, most businesses require a valid student ID to verify your status before offering discounts.

Healthcare & Wellness

Does UniSavvy provide mental health resources?
Yes. We list mental health services provided by your school and external free resources for students.
How do I choose a student health plan?
Use our health comparison tool in UniSavvy to compare options based on coverage, price, and provider network.

University Life

What services does the university provide?
Most universities offer tutoring, counseling, career services, and academic advising. UniSavvy links you to them directly.
How do I find student events?
The Events section of UniSavvy connects you to upcoming workshops, socials, and info sessions on your campus.

Using UniSavvy

Is UniSavvy free?
Yes. UniSavvy is completely free for students and supported by partnerships with educational institutions.
How do I report a problem or bug?
Use the Help section in the app to submit feedback or report a technical issue directly to our team.
