Student Healthcare Resources

Find affordable insurance and mental health support

Affordable Insurance Options

Health insurance is essential for students to stay healthy and protected from unexpected medical costs. Here are some affordable options to explore:

  • University Plans: Many universities offer health insurance tailored for students. Check with your campus health services.
  • Medicaid: If you have a low income, you may qualify for free or low-cost coverage through Medicaid.
  • Marketplace Plans: Visit HealthCare.gov to find affordable plans and see if you qualify for subsidies.
  • Parent's Plan: If you're under 26, you might still be eligible to stay on your parent's health insurance plan.

Mental Health Resources

Your mental health is just as important as your physical health. Here are some resources available to students:

  • Campus Counseling Services: Most universities offer free or low-cost counseling services for students.
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a trained crisis counselor 24/7.
  • Therapy Apps: Apps like BetterHelp and Talkspace provide remote therapy sessions at student-friendly prices.
  • Student Support Groups: Join on-campus or online peer support groups to connect with others facing similar challenges.
